Number of available software frameworks, libraries and tools grows exponentially. This site aims to provide some metrics to make it easier to orient oneself in this environment of rapid growth.

Various metrics from publicly available sources are gathered and exposed in a format convenient for human consumption. The idea is to facilitate informed decision-making process by individuals and companies who may find this kind of information handy.

An attempt is made to discover and disclose company affiliations where possible and identify driving organizational forces behind various technological trends. Affiliations of committers, company engineering blogs and other publicly available sources are used for that purpose.
